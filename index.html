Rules:

Bracket selection

The upper bracket is open to everyone, but it is recommended for those who have achieved either:

  • GENOSIDE 2018 段位認定 発狂五段 (Insane 5th) and above;
  • IIDX Kaiden;

While the lower bracket is open only to those who have either:

  • IIDX Chuuden or below;
  • GENOSIDE 2018 段位認定 発狂四段 (Insane 4th) or below;
Eventual other brackets can be joined by everyone.

Score submission

Scores must be submitted on the BMS Europe discord in the tournaments scoreposting thread in the scores channel. Feel free to ping me (@fresco69w) to death if you see that your score is not updated in a few hours (it is if your score has been reacted to).

Game client and options

Any client can be used. MIRROR, RANDOM (random trainer included), R-RANDOM and S-RANDOM are allowed.

Ranking

Ranking is decided based on a rating that comes from a formula which takes in account the amount of players participating and emphasizes the player ranking in the individual charts and the total score. You can find the details on the tournament calculation spreadsheet here.

FAQ

Q. Why these charts??
A. All the charts chosen follow a theme that changes every month.

Q. Where do I get the charts?
A. You can find the dl on the ranking page for the current season. (Check to burger menu on the top left!)

A. The homepage of this website is table! You can put it in your client to find the current tournament charts quickly. If for some reason you're looking for older tournaments charts, check the archive section on the hamburger menu on the top left.
